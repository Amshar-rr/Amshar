01Who I Am

About Me

My name is Amshar Jawsak people call me Amshar, a 17 year old student from Sri Lanka. I was born in 2009 in Thihariya, and from a very young age I had a drive to push myself further than expected.

My early education began at Crescent International School (CIS), Thihariya, in the Montessori batch of 2013/2014. That is where my love for learning first started growing.

In 2019, I sat the national scholarship exam and scored 167 marks, earning me a place at the prestigious DS Senanayake College, Colombo 07. Six remarkable years there, all the way through to my O/Levels in 2025/2026.

Now I am at OSDEM Dickwella, deep in my A/Levels, focused on Biology. I am someone who values quiet focus and personal reflection.

Every step has been intentional. This site is a small window into my life, where I have been and where I am going.

Life Journey

2009 — The Beginning
Born in Thihariya
Thihariya, Gampaha District, Sri Lanka

Thihariya is a town situated in the Gampaha District of Sri Lanka. It is known as one of the good cities in Gampaha, where many educated people have studied and grown up, helping to develop the town over the years. It carries a natural beauty that simply cannot be described in words. Insha Allah, I also want to be a part of developing my town if I get a chance to do so one day.

The first classroom. CIS in Thihariya is where the love of learning was born. Those early years built the discipline and curiosity that carried everything forward. It was the foundation of everything that followed.

My Primary Education
Dharussalam Primary School & Al-Badriya Maha Vidyalaya

I had the chance to study at both Dharussalam Primary School, Thihariya and Al-Badriya Maha Vidyalaya, Kahatowita for my primary education. While at Al-Badriya Maha Vidyalaya, I sat the Grade 5 scholarship exam just as all students do at that stage. I scored 167 marks, which opened the door to one of the most prestigious schools in Sri Lanka. Hard work had spoken for itself.
